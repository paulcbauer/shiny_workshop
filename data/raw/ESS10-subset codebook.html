News about politics and current affairs, watching, reading or listening, in minutes

ASK ALL
On a typical day, about how much time do you spend watching, reading or listening to news about politics and current affairs?
Please give your answer in hours and minutes. INTERVIEWER: If no time spent, enter 00 00. TYPE IN DURATION

8888 Don't know*
